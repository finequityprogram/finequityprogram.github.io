Complete the quiz to get credit for this module! Please note that the free response reflection question needs a minimum of 100 characters. NOTE: These questions refer to the example credit report you went through in the module! We encourage you to look back at the report while answering these questions.





















Reflection: Below are a list of things that can show up on a credit report and will likely result in a landlords or landlord deciding that they should reject your application:

  • Recent late payments (especially within the last 6 months)
  • Serious missed or late payments. Missed payments are serious if it has been more than 90 days late.
  • Maxing out a credit card/ using the entire credit limit
  • High utilization rate
  • Having multiple credit cards that all are highly used
  • Currently borrowing a significant amount of debt
  • Very little credit history or a short credit history
  • Many recent hard inquiries
