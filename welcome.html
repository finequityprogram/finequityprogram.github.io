Thank you for signing up for finEQUITY's financial education program. Throughout this mini-course, we'll cover a number of topics to help you understand credit. For instance, you will learn about the pros and cons of borrowing, credit scores, and credit reports, and how credit can affect your family members, friends, or yourself.

In fact, you might be excited because being able to have a good credit reputation can be a great asset to you and your family. It can help you achieve your goals, realize your vision, and create opportunities for yourself and your loved ones. Credit is a tool, and like any tool, it's important to know the proper and safest ways to use it. We hope that, by the end of this course, you will feel more confident, prepared, and informed about how to manage credit and your credit reputation.



If you have never participated in a financial education program before, don't worry! The hardest part is getting started, and you've already done that by enrolling.

In terms of logistics, this mini-course is structured into 8 modules, which you can navigate to on the sidebar. At the end of each module, there is a quiz and reflection for you to complete to get credit for the module; a finEQUITY member will then contact you within the week with feedback. After finishing all the modules, you will be eligible to receive a certificate of achievement.

Let's get started!
